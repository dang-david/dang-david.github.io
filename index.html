Welcome to League of Legends

A fast-paced 5v5 strategy game where two teams battle to destroy the enemy Nexus.

Objective & Map Basics

League of Legends is played on a map called Summoner’s Rift. Two teams of five players start on opposite sides of the map and fight to destroy the enemy Nexus, the crystal structure at the center of each base. To get there, teams push through three lanes—top, middle, and bottom—while destroying enemy turrets and inhibitors and defending their own.

Between the lanes lies the jungle, an area full of neutral monsters that give gold, experience, and powerful buffs when defeated. Controlling lanes, vision, and jungle objectives is what separates coordinated teams from disorganized ones.

Quick Facts

  • 5v5 multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA).
  • Main map: Summoner’s Rift with 3 lanes and a jungle.
  • Over 160 unique champions with different playstyles.
  • Primary goal: destroy the enemy Nexus before they destroy yours.

Champion Basics

Each player controls a single champion with a distinct kit of abilities. Champions gain experience to level up and unlock stronger spells, and earn gold by killing minions, monsters, and enemy champions. That gold is spent on items that increase damage, durability, or utility.

Over the course of a match, champions scale from weak early game characters into powerful late game threats. Teamwork, positioning, and objective control determine whether your team converts that power into a win.

New Player Tips

  1. Last-hit minions: only the final hit gives you gold.
  2. Watch the minimap: check it often to avoid surprise ganks.
  3. Start with simple champions: focus on learning one role at a time.
  4. Play for objectives: dragons, turrets, and Baron win more games than kills.
